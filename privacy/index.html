Chihu for NeoDB Privacy Policy

Last updated april 12, 2025

The Chihu for NeoDB app does not collect or process any personal information from its users. The app is used to connect to third-party NeoDB servers that may or may not collect personal information and are not covered by this privacy policy. Each third-party NeoDB server comes equipped with its own privacy policy that can be viewed through the app or through that server's website.

Key Points Summary

  • NeoDB Access: Our app requires access to your NeoDB account to provide its core functionality. We only store your authentication token locally on your device.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We collect only the minimum data necessary for the app to function. All data is stored locally on your device.
  • Your Control: You maintain complete control over your data and can revoke access or delete all local data at any time.

NeoDB Account Access

Our application requires access to your NeoDB account for the following specific purposes:

  • To allow you to browse and track your media collection
  • To enable social features like following users and sharing reviews
  • To provide the core features of our service

Important notes about our NeoDB access:

  • We store your authentication token securely on your device
  • We do not store your password
  • We do not share your authentication information with any third parties
  • All operations are performed through the official NeoDB API

Data Storage and Privacy

We are committed to maintaining minimal data collection. This means:

  • We only collect data necessary for the app to function
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not share any information with third parties
  • We do not use any third-party analytics services
  • You can delete all local data at any time

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: contact@chihu.app.
